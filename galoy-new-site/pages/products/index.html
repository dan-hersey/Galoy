Two Implementation Paths

The Galoy Stack can run as your primary core or as a sidecar alongside legacy systems.

Sidecar Model

Legacy software wasn't designed for digital assets. Our Sidecar Model plugs into your existing core system to open new streams of revenue.

Perfect for existing banks that want to add Bitcoin capabilities without replacing their core.
