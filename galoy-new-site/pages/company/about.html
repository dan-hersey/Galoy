About Galoy

We're building the future of banking by developing financial infrastructure on top of the Bitcoin network.

Our Mission

At Galoy, we are building the future of banking by developing a financial infrastructure stack on top of the Bitcoin network. Our platform is designed to foster collaboration and enable institutions to build on Bitcoin.

With it, we enable institutions to connect to and offer financial services on Bitcoin.

What We've Built

Lana

A Bitcoin-backed lending platform designed to enable financial institutions to offer bitcoin-backed loans.

Learn more →

Cala

A core accounting ledger providing double-entry bookkeeping for both BTC and fiat currencies with full audit capabilities.

Learn more →

Bria

A payments bridge providing secure, scalable interfaces between Bitcoin, Lightning Network, and enterprise applications.

Learn more →

Blink Wallet

A widely used custodial Lightning wallet that demonstrates our infrastructure in action, along with Stablesats for synthetic USD stability.

Visit Blink →
