About Galoy

Galoy builds core banking and payments infrastructure that enables traditional and de novo banks to launch products on Bitcoin. The company's technology stack includes Lana (Bitcoin-backed lending platform), Cala (core accounting ledger), and Bria (Bitcoin payments bridge).

Galoy is ISO 27001 certified and works with financial institutions globally to bring Bitcoin capabilities to regulated banking. The company offers two implementation paths: the Sidecar Model for existing banks adding Bitcoin alongside their current core, and Primary Core for new Bitcoin-first institutions.

Founded by Bitcoin and fintech industry veterans, Galoy is backed by leading investors including Hivemind Ventures, Valor Equity Partners, and Kingsway Capital.
