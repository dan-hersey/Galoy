Bitcoin Banking, Without Replacing Your Core

The Sidecar Model allows banks to add Bitcoin capabilities as a parallel system, without forcing digital assets into legacy cores.

Most banks don't want to rip and replace their core banking system to support Bitcoin. They don't need to.

Galoy is designed to operate as a Bitcoin-native sidecar, integrating cleanly alongside an existing core banking stack to enable Bitcoin services—starting with custody and Bitcoin-backed lending—without disrupting legacy systems, payment rails, or compliance workflows.

What Is the Sidecar Model?

The sidecar model allows banks to add Bitcoin capabilities as a parallel system, rather than forcing digital assets into legacy cores that were never designed to support them.

In this architecture:

  • The existing core continues to handle fiat accounts, payments, compliance, and reporting
  • Galoy operates as a dedicated Bitcoin ledger and transaction engine
  • Systems integrate via APIs and payment hubs already in place

This lets banks add Bitcoin capabilities—without compromising control, compliance, or safety.

Where Galoy Fits in the Stack

Galoy integrates alongside the core at three critical layers: ledger, asset management, and settlement.

Core Capabilities

Bitcoin Subledger

BTC balances, custody, and collateral tracking

Galoy provides a dedicated Bitcoin subledger that mirrors the rigor of a bank's general ledger—tracking BTC balances, ownership, and movements in real time, with full auditability.

Why it matters:

  • Keeps Bitcoin accounting separate from fiat ledgers
  • Enables accurate BTC balance reporting
  • Foundation for lending, custody, and payments

Custody

Bank-grade Bitcoin custody foundations

Galoy integrates with secure Bitcoin custody architectures, enabling banks to hold and manage BTC with the same governance, controls, and reporting standards applied to other high-value assets.

Why it matters:

  • Enables qualified custody and institutional trust
  • Supports multi-sig and policy-driven controls
  • Avoids rehypothecation and opaque risk

Bitcoin-Backed Lending

Collateralized lending using BTC

Galoy enables Bitcoin-backed lending by managing BTC collateral, real-time LTV monitoring, margin thresholds, and liquidation logic—without forcing banks to rebuild their loan systems.

Why it matters:

  • Bitcoin becomes usable collateral, not just speculative
  • Loans behave like traditional secured products
  • Risk managed continuously, not manually

Transaction Processing

Bitcoin & Lightning settlement

Galoy handles Bitcoin and Lightning transaction processing separately from legacy payment rails, while integrating cleanly into existing reporting and reconciliation workflows.

Why it matters:

  • 24/7 settlement without correspondent delays
  • Lightning enables instant, low-cost payments
  • No disruption to ACH, wire, or card systems

How This Works in Practice

With the sidecar model, Bitcoin services integrate seamlessly into your existing operations.

2
