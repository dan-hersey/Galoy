Yield Risk Disclosure

"The yield comes from lending activity. That means risk."

How Vault Yield Works

When you deposit Bitcoin in your Savings Vault, Bitibank lends a portion to qualified institutional borrowers. The interest they pay is passed to you as yield.

This is not free money. It carries risk. If borrowers default, your principal could be affected.

Risk Factors

Counterparty Risk
Borrowers may fail to repay loans. Bitibank requires over-collateralization but extreme market conditions could result in losses.
Smart Contract Risk
Some lending protocols use smart contracts. Bugs or exploits could affect funds.
Liquidity Risk
In extreme market conditions, withdrawals may be delayed if loans cannot be recalled immediately.
Regulatory Risk
Regulatory changes could affect lending programs or yield rates.

Our Protections

All loans are over-collateralized at minimum 130%
Lending limited to 40% of total vault deposits
Insurance reserve fund covering up to 10% of deposits
Quarterly independent audits published publicly
