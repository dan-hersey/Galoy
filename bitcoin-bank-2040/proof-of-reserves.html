What This Proves

Every quarter, Bitibank publishes cryptographic proof that we hold at least 100% of customer deposits in reserve. This isn't a promise — it's verifiable on the Bitcoin blockchain.

Any user can verify their balance is included in the proof without revealing their identity or balance to others.
